THE JOURNEY BEGINS

Flying through Moroccan sand.

Kacem Hamsas, a Moroccan rally driver, began his career in 2018. Starting as a co-pilot, he transitioned to a pilot in 2021, quickly making a name for himself in both local and international rallies. With a strong drive for success, Kacem continues to push the limits in the world of rally racing.

Focus is what separates a driver from a champion. It's not just about seeing the road — it's about feeling every inch of it before it even appears.

"With the helmet on, he becomes the machine. With it off, the heart behind the wheel comes to life. Casque sur la tête, il devient la machine. Une fois retiré, c'est le cœur derrière le volant qui prend vie"

Kacem Hamsas approaches rally racing with a mindset rooted in discipline and clarity. Every maneuver is calculated, every acceleration purposeful. His focus isn't just on winning - it's on mastering the terrain, communicating with the car, and respecting the challenge ahead. For him, rallying is not just a sport, it's a language of instinct and control.

Behind the wheel

For Kacem, rallying isn't just a race — it's a mindset. Each moment in the cockpit is a balance between precision and instinct. He sees every challenge as an opportunity to learn, adapt, and push further. It's not only about the finish line, but the story written between start and stop.

Career highlights

  • 2018-2020 Began as a co-pilot at age 14, navigating alongside his father, a former rally driver.
  • 2021 Took the wheel for the first time, competing as a pilot in local rally events.
  • 2022: Participated in the legendary Rallye du Maroc, gaining valuable desert racing experience.
  • 2023: Achieved 2nd place in the M'Hamid Express Rallye, marking a breakthrough year.
  • 2023: Finished 2nd in the Sand Express Rallye with veteran co-pilot Jean Brucy.
  • 2024: Returned to M'Hamid Express, this time winning 1st place with Paul Duramee as co-pilot.
  • 2025: Secured the internationally competitive Carta Rallye in the internationally competitive Carta Rallye.
